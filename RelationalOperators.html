Relational Operators
Relational operators are used to check the relation between two values or variables. If the relation is true, they give back true else false.

Consider three variables.

a=10
b=40
c=40

Following are the relational operators, their meaning and values for operation on above variables.

==
Meaning : is equal to
a==b gives false
b==c gives true

!=
Meaning : is not equal to
a==b gives true
b==c gives false

>
Meaning : greater than
a>b gives false
b>a gives true

<
Meaning : less than
a<b gives true
b<a gives false

>=
Meaning : greater than or equal to
a>=b gives false
b>=c gives true

<=
Meaning : less than or equal to
a<=b gives true
b<=c gives true
